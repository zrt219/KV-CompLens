AI for Student-Athlete Success — 50-Minute Workshop Outline

Host-ready agenda and timing guide

Workshop Purpose

This 50-minute session gives student-athletes a practical introduction to using AI responsibly for career readiness, personal brand development, digital reputation, and life after sport.

Detailed Run of Show

TimeSegmentSpeaker FocusParticipant Activity
0–5Welcome + Why This MattersIntroduce UMATTR Career Circle. Share a brief athlete story about pressure, transition, and finishing strong.Write one word that describes how they feel about life after sport.
5–15AI Basics for AthletesExplain AI simply: a tool for drafting, organizing, practicing, brainstorming, and planning — not a replacement for judgment or character.Identify one area where AI could save time.
15–25Career ReadinessShow how athletic skills translate to workplace skills: discipline, teamwork, resilience, leadership, preparation, time management, and coachability.Complete the transferable skills section.
25–35Personal Brand + ReputationDiscuss digital reputation, NIL-era choices, authenticity, and how athletes can build a professional presence.Complete a personal brand sentence.
35–45AI Practice LabModel safe prompts for resumes, interviews, networking, and 30-day planning.Choose one prompt and customize it.
45–50Final Challenge + CloseInvite athletes to create a 30-day next step and join UMATTR Career Circle for continued support.Write one action they will take this week.

Key Messages to Repeat

  • AI is a tool. Your judgment, values, and voice still matter.
  • Your athlete skills already have career value.
  • Your digital reputation can open or close doors.
  • Life after sport should be prepared for before sport ends.

Materials Needed

  • Participant workbook
  • Worksheet packet
  • AI prompt cheat sheet
  • Slides or screen-share outline
  • Chat box or Q&A space inside UMATTR Career Circle

Optional Closing Script

You are more than your last race, game, injury, statistic, or season. Your discipline can become your career advantage. Your story can become your strength. And with the right tools, including AI used wisely, you can build a future beyond sport with confidence and purpose.
