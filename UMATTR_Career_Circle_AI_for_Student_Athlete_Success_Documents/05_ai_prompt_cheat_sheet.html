AI Prompt Cheat Sheet

10 safe prompts every student-athlete can use

How to Use This Cheat Sheet

Copy a prompt, replace the bracketed sections, and review the answer carefully. AI can help you draft and organize, but your final work should sound like you and tell the truth.

10 Safe AI Prompts

  1. Resume: Help me turn my experience as a [sport] athlete into 5 resume bullet points for [career field]. Do not exaggerate.
  2. Interview: Ask me 5 practice interview questions and help me answer using examples from athletics and school.
  3. Networking: Write a short professional message to introduce myself to an alumni mentor in [industry].
  4. LinkedIn: Help me write a LinkedIn About section as a student-athlete studying [major] and interested in [field].
  5. Time management: Create a weekly schedule that balances training, classes, recovery, studying, and career development.
  6. Personal brand: Help me write a personal brand statement that reflects my values, sport, leadership, and career goals.
  7. Career exploration: Suggest 10 careers that connect my major in [major], my athletic experience, and my interest in [interest].
  8. Email: Draft a polite email asking a professor, coach, or mentor for advice about internships.
  9. Content planning: Give me 10 professional social media post ideas that show leadership, discipline, community, and career growth.
  10. 30-day plan: Build a 30-day action plan to prepare for life after sport, including networking, resume work, and reflection.

Safety Rules

  • Do not upload private personal information, medical information, passwords, IDs, or confidential team information.
  • Do not ask AI to do academic work you are required to complete yourself.
  • Check all facts before using AI output.
  • Edit everything so it sounds human and authentic.
