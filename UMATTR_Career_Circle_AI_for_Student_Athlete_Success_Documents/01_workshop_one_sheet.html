AI for Student-Athlete Success — Workshop One-Sheet

Career Readiness, Personal Brand, and Life After Sport

Program Overview

AI for Student-Athlete Success is a 50-minute live workshop delivered through UMATTR Career Circle, our LinkedIn/Zoom-style social media platform for live learning, community connection, networking, and practical growth support.

This workshop helps student-athletes understand how to use AI responsibly for career readiness, personal brand clarity, digital reputation, NIL-era decision-making, and life after sport.

Who This Is For

  • NCAA, university, college, high school, club, and elite student-athletes
  • Student-athlete development departments and career centers
  • Coaches, athletic directors, team captains, and leadership groups
  • Athletes preparing for internships, graduation, NIL opportunities, or career transition

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand what AI can and cannot do for student-athletes.
  • Translate athletic experience into professional skills for resumes, interviews, and networking.
  • Use AI prompts for career planning, communication, organization, and confidence-building.
  • Build a personal brand that supports trust, opportunity, and digital reputation.
  • Create a simple 30-day action plan for life after sport.

50-Minute Format

  • Certificate template

Speaker

Lornette Daye is the founder of UMATTR, a former elite sprinter, Canadian National Team athlete, coach, mentor, entrepreneur, and public speaker. Her message helps athletes finish strong, build confidence, and prepare for success beyond competition.
