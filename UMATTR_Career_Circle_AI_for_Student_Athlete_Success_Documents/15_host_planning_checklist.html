Host Planning Checklist

Pre-event planning checklist for UMATTR Career Circle workshops

Purpose

This checklist helps the host prepare for a smooth 50-minute UMATTR Career Circle workshop experience.

Before the Event

  • [ ] Confirm date, time, and time zone.
  • [ ] Confirm audience size and athlete profile.
  • [ ] Confirm platform: UMATTR Career Circle, Zoom, campus platform, or hybrid.
  • [ ] Share event flyer and registration information.
  • [ ] Send participant workbook and worksheet packet before the session.
  • [ ] Confirm speaker introduction and host name.
  • [ ] Confirm whether the session is recorded.
  • [ ] Confirm accessibility needs and accommodations.

During the Event

  • [ ] Open the room 10 minutes early.
  • [ ] Welcome participants.
  • [ ] Introduce Lornette Daye using the speaker introduction sheet.
  • [ ] Encourage chat/Q&A participation.
  • [ ] Provide workbook link during the session.
  • [ ] Share feedback form at the end.

After the Event

  • [ ] Send follow-up email and resources.
  • [ ] Send certificate if applicable.
  • [ ] Collect feedback and testimonials.
  • [ ] Schedule optional follow-up workshop or consulting call.
  • [ ] Review next steps for a three-part series or semester package.
