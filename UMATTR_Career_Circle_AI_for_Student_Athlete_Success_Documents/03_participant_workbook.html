Part 2: AI in Simple Words

AI can help you draft, organize, practice, brainstorm, research, and plan. AI should not replace your voice, truth, values, effort, academic integrity, or personal judgment.

Healthy AI Use

  • Use AI to think better, not to avoid thinking.
  • Never put private personal information into a tool without permission and awareness.
  • Check facts and revise anything AI creates.
  • Use your own voice before submitting, posting, or sending.
