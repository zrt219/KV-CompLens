The Opportunity

Student-athletes are navigating academic pressure, athletic demands, digital reputation, NIL-era decisions, career uncertainty, and the fast-growing impact of artificial intelligence. They need practical guidance that connects technology, character, communication, personal brand, and life-after-sport planning.

The UMATTR Solution

UMATTR delivers a practical 50-minute workshop through UMATTR Career Circle, our LinkedIn/Zoom-style social media platform for live learning and community support. The session gives student-athletes simple AI tools and reflective exercises they can use immediately.

Workshop Objectives

  • Build responsible AI awareness.
  • Strengthen career readiness and transferable skill language.
  • Support personal brand and digital reputation planning.
  • Encourage confidence and identity beyond sport.
  • Create a practical 30-day action plan.
